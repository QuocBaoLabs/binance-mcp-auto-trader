CHIEN LUOC 1 - SFP (SWING FAILURE PATTERN)
==========================================

File nay dien giai logic chien luoc "1. SFP" trong bot trade.
Muc tieu la de ban doc duoc bot dang tim gi, vao lenh khi nao, SL/TP tinh ra sao,
va vi sao co luc co tin hieu nhung bot khong vao lenh.


1. SFP LA GI?
-------------

SFP la viet tat cua Swing Failure Pattern.

Y tuong:
- Gia pha qua mot dinh/day swing gan nhat de quet thanh khoan.
- Sau do gia dong nen quay nguoc lai vao trong vung cu.
- Neu quet dinh roi dong lai ben duoi dinh: day la Bearish SFP, uu tien SHORT.
- Neu quet day roi dong lai ben tren day: day la Bullish SFP, uu tien LONG.

Noi gon:
- Bearish SFP = gia chich len tren swing high, nhung dong nen lai duoi swing high.
- Bullish SFP = gia chich xuong duoi swing low, nhung dong nen lai tren swing low.

Bot chi dung nen da dong de phan tich. Nen dang chay/live candle se bi loai ra.


2. THAM SO QUAN TRONG
---------------------

2.1. Pivot lookback / sfpLen

Trong UI, tham so nay la "Pivot lookback".
Mac dinh thuong la 5.

Bot dung sfpLen de tim swing high va swing low gan nhat.

Vi du sfpLen = 5:
- Swing high hop le khi high cua nen pivot cao hon cac high ben trai theo do dai lookback,
  va nen sau pivot khong tao high cao hon no.
- Swing low hop le khi low cua nen pivot thap hon cac low ben trai theo do dai lookback,
  va nen sau pivot khong tao low thap hon no.

Tang sfpLen:
- Swing chat hon, it tin hieu hon.
- Tin hieu co the "sach" hon nhung cho lau hon.

Giam sfpLen:
- De co tin hieu hon.
- Nhieu tin hieu nho va nhieu nhieu hon.


3. CACH BOT PHAT HIEN SFP
-------------------------

Bot lay danh sach nen cua symbol/timeframe dang quet.
Sau do loai nen dang hinh thanh, chi giu nen da dong.

3.1. Tim swing high gan nhat

Bot quet tu gan hien tai ve qua khu de tim pivot high.
Neu tim duoc swing high, bot lay gia high do lam swingPrice.

3.2. Tim swing low gan nhat

Bot quet tu gan hien tai ve qua khu de tim pivot low.
Neu tim duoc swing low, bot lay gia low do lam swingPrice.

3.3. Bearish SFP

Dieu kien:
- Da co swing high.
- Nen SFP co high > swing high.
- Nen SFP dong cua close < swing high.

Y nghia:
- Gia quet len tren dinh cu de lay thanh khoan buy-side.
- Nhung cuoi nen bi day nguoc xuong.
- Bot hieu la that bai khi pha dinh, co kha nang dao chieu giam.

Ket qua:
- Huong tin hieu: BEARISH.
- Lenh neu vao: SHORT.
- swingPrice = gia swing high bi quet.
- oppositeLevel = day thap nhat tu sau swing high den truoc nen SFP.

3.4. Bullish SFP

Dieu kien:
- Da co swing low.
- Nen SFP co low < swing low.
- Nen SFP dong cua close > swing low.

Y nghia:
- Gia quet xuong duoi day cu de lay thanh khoan sell-side.
- Nhung cuoi nen bi keo nguoc len.
- Bot hieu la that bai khi pha day, co kha nang dao chieu tang.

Ket qua:
- Huong tin hieu: BULLISH.
- Lenh neu vao: LONG.
- swingPrice = gia swing low bi quet.
- oppositeLevel = dinh cao nhat tu sau swing low den truoc nen SFP.

3.5. Tin hieu "confirmed"

Bot co truong confirmed.

Voi Bearish SFP:
- confirmed = true neu gia dong cua da pha xuong duoi oppositeLevel.

Voi Bullish SFP:
- confirmed = true neu gia dong cua da pha len tren oppositeLevel.

Confirmed khong co nghia la chac chan thang.
No chi noi rang gia da di tiep qua muc xac nhan sau khi quet thanh khoan.


4. ENTRY DUOC TINH NHU THE NAO?
-------------------------------

Voi chien luoc SFP, bot uu tien lay entry bang gia thi truong moi nhat tu Binance ticker.

Neu khong lay duoc ticker:
- Bot fallback ve close cua nen da dong gan nhat.

Vi vay entry tren chart/bang co the khac mot chut so voi gia nen SFP.
Neu ban bat "Vao lenh Market", lenh vao se theo gia thi truong thuc te, nen gia khop that tren Binance co the lech nhe so voi entry record.


5. STOP LOSS DUOC TINH NHU THE NAO?
-----------------------------------

SL cua SFP rat truc tiep: dat tai cuc tri cua nen SFP.

Voi LONG / Bullish SFP:
- SL = low cua nen SFP.
- Ly do: neu gia quay lai pha low nay, setup quet day that bai.

Voi SHORT / Bearish SFP:
- SL = high cua nen SFP.
- Ly do: neu gia quay lai pha high nay, setup quet dinh that bai.

Vi du LONG:
- Entry = 1.0000.
- Nen SFP co low = 0.9900.
- SL = 0.9900.

Vi du SHORT:
- Entry = 1.0000.
- Nen SFP co high = 1.0100.
- SL = 1.0100.


6. TAKE PROFIT DUOC TINH NHU THE NAO?
-------------------------------------

Bot co 2 che do tinh TP cho SFP.

6.1. TP theo RR mac dinh

Neu "TP SFP (%)" = 0, bot dung RR mac dinh.
Trong code hien tai, SFP_DEFAULT_TARGET_RR duoc dung de tinh khoang TP.

Cong thuc:
- riskDistance = abs(entry - SL)
- tpDistance = riskDistance * SFP_DEFAULT_TARGET_RR

Voi LONG:
- TP = entry + tpDistance

Voi SHORT:
- TP = entry - tpDistance

6.2. TP theo phan tram co dinh

Neu "TP SFP (%)" > 0:
- Bot khong dung RR mac dinh.
- Bot dung phan tram TP rieng.

Cong thuc:
- tpDistance = entry * (TP_percent / 100)

Voi LONG:
- TP = entry + tpDistance

Voi SHORT:
- TP = entry - tpDistance

Vi du:
- Entry = 1.0000.
- TP SFP (%) = 0.5.
- tpDistance = 1.0000 * 0.5 / 100 = 0.005.
- LONG TP = 1.0050.
- SHORT TP = 0.9950.


7. CHAM DIEM SETUP SFP
----------------------

Sau khi phat hien SFP, bot khong vao lenh ngay.
Bot tao mot "plan" va cham diem rule.

Tong diem duoc quy ve 0-100.
Tin hieu dat dieu kien khi:
- score >= nguong toi thieu cua SFP.
- Khong co fail nghiem trong.
- So loi chat luong khong qua gioi han.

Cac nhom rule chinh gom:

7.1. Hinh dang setup / risk-shape

Bot kiem tra quan he Entry - SL - TP co hop ly khong.

LONG:
- SL phai nam duoi entry.
- TP phai nam tren entry.

SHORT:
- SL phai nam tren entry.
- TP phai nam duoi entry.

Neu sai huong, bot bo qua.

7.2. Stop distance

Bot xem SL co qua xa entry khong.

Neu SL qua xa:
- R/R xau.
- Lo khi cham SL qua lon.
- De bi risk manager chan.

7.3. Stop theo timeframe

Bot co gioi han do xa SL theo tung timeframe.
Khung nho nhu 1m/3m can SL ngan hon.
Khung lon co the chap nhan SL rong hon.

7.4. ATR cap

Bot dung ATR de do bien dong.
Neu SL lon qua nhieu so voi ATR, setup bi xem la qua rong/khong dep.

7.5. Risk/Reward

Bot tinh RR = khoang TP / khoang SL.
Neu RR qua thap, bot co the bo qua.

7.6. Liquidation buffer

Bot uoc tinh rui ro thanh ly theo leverage.
Neu SL nam qua gan vung nguy hiem, bot bo qua.

7.7. Entry chase

Neu gia hien tai da chay qua xa so voi setup, bot xem la duoi lenh.
Duoi lenh lam SL/TP xau di, nen co the bo qua.

7.8. Mau nen xac nhan

Neu chien luoc "Mau nen" cung bat, SFP co the duoc cham them diem hoi tu.

Neu mau nen cung huong SFP:
- Bot ghi "Hoi tu".
- Diem setup tot hon.

Neu mau nen nguoc huong SFP:
- Bot ghi "Xung dot".
- Setup yeu hon, co the bi bo qua.

Neu khong co mau nen:
- Khong fail nang.
- Chi thieu diem bonus.


8. QUYET DINH TRADE HAY SKIP
----------------------------

Bot co 2 ket qua:

8.1. TRADE

Nghia la setup du dieu kien theo rule.

Neu auto execute dang tat:
- Signal vao danh sach cho xac nhan.
- Ban co the bam "Vao lenh" thu cong.

Neu auto execute dang bat:
- Signal duoc dua vao queue.
- Bot doi so nen theo "sfpWaitCandles".
- Den gio thi moi thu vao lenh neu van con slot va risk ok.

8.2. SKIP

Nghia la setup co SFP nhung khong du dieu kien vao lenh.

Neu auto execute dang bat:
- Bot chuyen signal sang rejected.
- Chart se ghi ly do khong vao lenh.

Neu auto execute dang tat:
- Mot so signal van co the hien de ban xem/tu quyet dinh, tuy theo flow UI.


9. sfpWaitCandles - DOI THEM NEN TRUOC KHI VAO
----------------------------------------------

Neu auto execute bat va setup TRADE:
- Bot khong nhat thiet vao ngay.
- Bot tinh executeAfter = hien tai + sfpWaitCandles * do dai timeframe.

Vi du:
- Timeframe = 1m.
- sfpWaitCandles = 3.
- Signal dat luc 10:00.
- Bot se doi den khoang 10:03 roi moi xet vao lenh.

Neu sfpWaitCandles = 0:
- Bot co the dua vao queue gan nhu ngay lap tuc.

Muc dich cua wait:
- Giam vao qua som sau nen quet thanh khoan.
- Cho thi truong xac nhan them.
- Nhung doi lau qua thi co the miss lenh.


10. LOC TRUNG SETUP
-------------------

Bot co co che khong lap lai setup qua gan.

No so sanh:
- Symbol.
- Direction.
- Swing price.
- Time window theo timeframe.

Neu setup qua giong signal gan day:
- Bot bo qua, khong tao signal moi.

Muc dich:
- Khong spam nhieu lenh cung mot setup.
- Khong vao lai lien tuc sau khi vua bi reject/ignored.


11. KHI NAO BOT GUI LENH THAT?
------------------------------

Bot chi gui lenh that khi tat ca dieu kien sau dung:

1. SFP duoc bat trong danh sach chien luoc.
2. Scanner dang bat.
3. Auto trade / auto execute dang bat neu muon bot tu vao.
4. Khong o READ_ONLY.
5. DRY_RUN=false neu muon lenh that.
6. API Binance hop le.
7. Symbol/timeframe con nam trong danh sach quet.
8. Setup co decision = TRADE.
9. Den thoi gian executeAfter neu co wait candles.
10. Con slot lenh theo maxOpenPositions.
11. Khong trung symbol/khong trung setup dang co exposure.
12. Risk manager khong chan.


12. KHI GUI LENH, BOT DAT NHUNG LENH NAO?
-----------------------------------------

Voi SFP thuong:
- Entry: MARKET neu "Vao lenh Market" bat, nguoc lai LIMIT.
- SL: STOP_MARKET closePosition=true.
- TP: TAKE_PROFIT_MARKET hoac protected TP theo flow order.

Neu co trailing stop duoc cau hinh rieng:
- Bot co the dung trailing stop thay/di kem logic TP theo cau hinh.

Tat ca lenh bao ve phai la reduceOnly/closePosition de khong mo them vi the nguoc chieu.


13. RISK MANAGER CHAN LENH VI NHUNG LY DO NAO?
----------------------------------------------

Day la cac ly do rat hay gap:

13.1. Khong du so du

Vi du:
- Khong du margin.
- Khong du buffer phi.
- Binance bao "Margin is insufficient".

13.2. Notional nho hon 5 USDT

Binance Futures yeu cau notional toi thieu.
Neu margin * leverage qua nho, bot se bi chan.

13.3. SL qua xa

Bot tinh:
- slMovePct = abs(entry - SL) / entry * 100
- slLossOfMarginPct = slMovePct * leverage

Gioi han hien tai:
- Cross: toi da 25% ky quy lenh.
- Isolated: toi da 35% ky quy lenh.

Vi du:
- SL cach entry 1%.
- Leverage 20x.
- Neu cham SL, mat khoang 20% ky quy lenh.

Neu vuot gioi han, bot chan.

13.4. RR qua thap

Neu TP gan hon SL, RR thap, bot co the chan.

13.5. SL/TP sai phia

LONG:
- SL phai duoi entry.
- TP phai tren entry.

SHORT:
- SL phai tren entry.
- TP phai duoi entry.

13.6. Da du slot lenh

Neu so vi the/lenh cho da cham maxOpenPositions, bot khong mo them.

13.7. Coin khong nam trong danh sach duoc phep

Neu symbol khong co trong allowed/watch list, bot bo qua.


14. DOC CHART SFP
-----------------

Tren chart SFP:
- ENTRY: gia bot du kien vao hoac gia record.
- SL: cuc tri cua nen SFP.
- TP: target tinh theo RR hoac %.
- Vung highlight: nen SFP/nen lien quan.
- Swing level: muc high/low bi quet.
- Neu chart ghi "KHONG VAO LENH": bot da tao setup nhung risk/rule chan.

Neu bam Live chart:
- Dashboard goi Binance lay nen moi nhat.
- Bot tao lai SVG tu du lieu live moi.
- Entry/SL/TP van la muc cua signal da luu, khong tu doi theo chart moi.


15. CAC TINH HUONG THUONG GAP
-----------------------------

15.1. Co tin hieu SFP nhung khong co lenh

Nguyen nhan co the:
- Score duoi nguong.
- SL qua xa.
- RR xau.
- Het slot.
- Khong du so du.
- Trung setup gan day.
- Auto execute tat.
- Dang doi sfpWaitCandles.

15.2. Entry tren chart khac gia khop Binance

Binh thuong.
Neu vao MARKET, gia khop theo thi truong that.
Record entry la gia bot tinh luc tao/execute signal.

15.3. SHORT ma lenh bao ve la BUY

Dung.
Vi SHORT muon dong vi the thi phai BUY.

15.4. LONG ma lenh bao ve la SELL

Dung.
Vi LONG muon dong vi the thi phai SELL.

15.5. Thay lệnh Stop Market / Mua voi SHORT

Day la SL/TP dong short.
Khong phai bot vao LONG moi, neu closePosition=true hoac reduceOnly=true.


16. KHUYEN NGHI CAU HINH SFP
----------------------------

Neu ban muon it lenh nhung chat hon:
- Tang Pivot lookback.
- Tang sfpWaitCandles.
- De TP theo RR, khong dat TP % qua gan.
- Giu leverage vua phai.
- Dung isolated neu so du nho va muon tach rui ro.

Neu ban muon nhieu lenh hon:
- Giam Pivot lookback.
- Giam sfpWaitCandles.
- Dung timeframe 1m/3m.
- Nhung phai chap nhan nhieu setup bi reject/nhieu nhieu hon.

Neu so du nho:
- Khong nen leverage qua cao.
- Nen de margin moi lenh nho hon tong kha dung chia cho so slot.
- Theo doi notional toi thieu 5 USDT.


17. TOM TAT NGAN GON
--------------------

SFP vao lenh theo logic:

1. Tim swing high/low gan nhat.
2. Cho gia quet qua swing.
3. Neu nen dong cua quay lai trong swing -> co SFP.
4. Huong:
   - Quet high roi dong duoi high -> SHORT.
   - Quet low roi dong tren low -> LONG.
5. Entry = gia ticker Binance moi nhat, fallback close nen.
6. SL = high/low cua nen SFP.
7. TP = theo RR mac dinh hoac theo TP SFP (%).
8. Cham diem rule.
9. Neu score dat va risk ok -> TRADE.
10. Neu auto bat -> doi sfpWaitCandles roi vao neu con slot/risk ok.
11. Neu khong dat -> SKIP/REJECT va chart ghi ly do.


18. DIEU CAN NHO
----------------

SFP la chien luoc bat that bai pha dinh/day.
No manh khi gap dung vung thanh khoan ro, volume/nen dao chieu tot, va SL ngan.

SFP yeu khi:
- Gia da chay xa moi vao.
- SL qua rong.
- TP gan hon SL.
- Dang trend manh va SFP chi la mot pullback nho.

Bot khong chi thay SFP la vao.
Bot phai di qua score, risk manager, slot, so du, va che do auto.
